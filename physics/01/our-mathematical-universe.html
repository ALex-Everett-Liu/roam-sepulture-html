Book Information

Author: Max Tegmark
Published: 2013
ISBN: 978-0-307-59980-3
Genre: Cosmology / Physics / Mathematics

A groundbreaking exploration of the ultimate nature of reality, proposing that our physical world is not merely described by mathematics, but is mathematics itself. Tegmark takes readers on a journey from the smallest scales of quantum mechanics to the largest cosmic structures, revealing how mathematical patterns underlie everything in our universe.
